About the Bash

A one-night Christmas celebration with Coldplay live in concert.

What is Coldplay Christmas Bash?

Coldplay Christmas Bash 2025 is a special holiday edition concert imagined as a colourful mix of Coldplay’s stadium energy and the cozy warmth of Christmas. The show blends a full-length Coldplay live performance with Christmas storytelling, visuals and interactive moments for the audience.

The night features custom visuals inspired by the Music of the Spheres tour, but re-designed with Christmas colours – red, green and gold. Fans receive LED wristbands that sync with the music, turning the entire arena into a moving light show. Snow cannons, confetti storms and surprise Christmas carols appear between songs to keep the crowd hyped and involved.

Along with the concert, there are festive zones around the venue including a hot chocolate bar, holiday photo booths, charity gift drop-off, and a “Wish Wall” where fans can write their Christmas wishes that light up on the big screens. The goal is simple – to end the year together with music, kindness and pure Christmas joy.
